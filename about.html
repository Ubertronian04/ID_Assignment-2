About

MOCHII EDU is an interactive educational game site that motivates students to learn while playing games. You can be anywhere in the world, on any smart devices and still be able to learn with MOCHII EDU!

It was made as part of a school project to create an interactive web application. This was created through a collaborative effort between 3 year-1 polytechnic students to create an educational web application tailored towards students whom are taking their PSLE, O'LEVELS/N'LEVELS.

We want to make learning fun for students. A site/application that has their learning materials and giving them flexibility to choose how they want to learn. Here at MOCHII EDU, students will find a fresh new way of learning through our classes in the form of games.

We hope you will enjoy what Mochii Edu has to offer!
